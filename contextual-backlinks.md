# Contextual Backlinks

One pattern I’ve been observing is that links behave differently depending on the surrounding content.

## Key insight
A link placed inside relevant, meaningful content tends to perform differently from a standalone link.

## Why this happens
- Context adds relevance signals
- Placement affects interpretation
- Standalone links often carry weaker intent
