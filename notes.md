## Structural SEO Notes

These notes explore several structural SEO concepts that influence how search engines interpret websites.

### Topics being explored

- Link architecture and authority flow
- Anchor text distribution patterns
- Internal linking hierarchy
- Topical clustering models
- Structural signals used by search engines

### Observation

In many cases, ranking volatility is not caused by optimization problems but by structural weaknesses in how pages relate to each other.

A well-structured site distributes authority more efficiently and allows search engines to better interpret topical relevance.
